# 3.PROJECT OVERVIEW(PART 1)
♦ Name  

Muzila Foundation  

♦ A brief history of the organization  

o It is a first hand  experience in impacting individuals and families to help improve their circumstances .Our impact ranges from crucial health  interventions and support to micro business training and mentoring .We believe in that we can break poverty cycles by nurturing individuals and supporting families , and help them realise their full potential .We work alongside those we serve ,helping them believe in themselves with dignity.


♦ Mission and Vision Statements 

o Mission  

is to provide care and support to innocent and vulnerable children ,and ensure that children have a safe environment. We strive to equip children and families with the tools  they need to overcome adversity and achieve lasting stability.  

Vision  

Our vision  create a strong financially secure and usefull educated family and create a legacy of love, integrity and purpose for generations to come .  

  

♦ TARGET AUDIENCE  

o Local communities  

o Stakeholders  

Families(elders, sibling) 

  

WEBSITE GOALS AND OBJECTIVES  

♦ Specific goals  

 o Promote togetherness , family unity  and celebrating family heritage and their differences . 

♦ Objectives  

o It is to empower these families to create their own futures .  

 

Goal 1 

Objectives (Actionable): 

Create engaging social media content such as  podcasts, videos, blogs, skits and mainly  focusing on family stories ,culture practices and traditions as a whole . 

Highlights celebrations ,family gathering ,workshops on online event calendar . 

Provides guide to practice unity activities for families at their homes. 

Form polls, quizzes to promote participation on family values. 

 

Goal 2 

Actionable Objectives: 

Offer workshops teaching on family leadership, conflict resolution and financial planning . 

Launch a resource hub with article experts advice and family planning tool . 

Share communication as peers including private group features. 

Create a program for mentoring experienced family members can guide younger ones. 

 

Goal 3 

Actionable Objectives: 

Run online awareness campaigns and asking people to participate to encourage trust . 

To promote events and campaigns collaborate with influencers or community leaders . 

Optimize the website search engine (SEO) using  keywords related to heritage and family  unity . 

Use social media platforms to share updates.
